About the System

A modern web-based platform for organized and efficient healthcare in Pulilan

The Pulilan Health Center Appointment and Patient Queuing Management System is a web-based platform designed to modernize healthcare services in the Municipality of Pulilan, Bulacan. It enables residents to schedule appointments online and monitor queue status in real time.

By digitizing appointment scheduling and queue management, the system helps reduce waiting time, improve patient flow, and support healthcare workers in delivering faster and more reliable medical services to the community.

Priority Lane

Special Services for Priority Patients

Senior Citizens and Persons with Disability (PWD) are served first

Senior Citizens

Residents aged 60 and above automatically receive priority queue numbers (P-) and are served ahead of the regular queue.

Persons with Disability (PWD)

Registered PWD patients receive priority lane access with P- queue numbers for faster service delivery.

How to Register Priority Status

Complete your profile and enable your Senior Citizen or PWD status under the Benefits section of your account.
